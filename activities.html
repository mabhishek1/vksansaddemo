Some Of Our Activities

" দরিদ্র নারায়ণ সেবা করা হলো নিজের মহিমা পরিবর্তন করা "

(Serving the poor is not only a duty but also a way to transform oneself.)

Pandemic Donations

Pandemic donations refer to the act of giving financial or material assistance to individuals or organizations affected by the COVID-19 pandemic, including medical supplies, food, shelter, and financial aid

Free Medical Camps

We organize and facilitate medical camps, which provide free or low-cost medical services, such as checkups, diagnostics, and treatments, to underserved communities in various locations, including rural and urban areas

Support During natural calamity

Support during natural calamity refers to the aid and assistance provided to those affected by natural disasters, such as floods, hurricanes, earthquakes, or wildfires. This can include providing food, shelter, medical aid, and other essential supplies to help people recover and rebuild their lives after such events.

Food distribution to the poor

Food distribution to the poor is a charitable activity that involves providing food to individuals or families who are facing economic hardships and cannot afford sufficient meals. This may include organizing food drives, distributing packaged meals, or partnering with local food banks and shelters to reach those in need.

Clothes and Essential donations

Clothes and essential donations refer to the act of donating clothing, personal care items, and other basic necessities to those in need, such as people experiencing homelessness, victims of natural disasters, or low-income families. This helps ensure that individuals have access to essential items that they may not be able to afford on their own.

About Us

At Vivekananda Sansad, we believe that community service and social responsibility are the cornerstones of a strong and prosperous society. That is why we have made it our mission to empower the marginalized and the vulnerable by providing them with the support they need to thrive.
