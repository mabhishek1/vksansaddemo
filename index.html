ABOUT Vivekananda Sansad

" দরিদ্র নারায়ণ সেবা করা হলো নিজের মহিমা পরিবর্তন করা "

(Serving the poor is not only a duty but also a way to transform oneself.)

Welcome to Vivekananda Sansad, a community organization based in Vivek Vill, dedicated to supporting the poor and the needy, promoting cultural programs, and organizing medical health camps

At Vivekananda Sansad, we believe that community service and social responsibility are the cornerstones of a strong and prosperous society. That is why we have made it our mission to empower the marginalized and the vulnerable by providing them with the support they need to thrive.

We organize a wide range of programs and initiatives to achieve our goals. Our cultural programs are designed to promote diversity and inclusivity, and to celebrate the rich heritage of our community. Our medical health camps provide free health checkups and medical services to those who cannot afford them, and our disaster relief efforts aim to support the government in times of need and provide food and shelter to those affected by natural calamities.

As a community organization, we believe in the power of collaboration and partnership. We work closely with local businesses, government agencies, and other community groups to maximize our impact and reach as many people as possible. We also welcome volunteers and supporters who share our vision and want to contribute to our mission.

Thank you for visiting our website and learning more about Vivekananda Sansad. We hope you will join us in our efforts to build a more equitable and compassionate world.
